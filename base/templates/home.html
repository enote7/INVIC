Welcome to Invic System

Invic System is your ultimate platform for video conferencing and chatting. Below is a quick user guide to help you navigate the platform and make the most out of its features.

Pages You Can Access

  • Signup: Create a new account to start using the platform.
  • Login: Access your account by logging in.
  • Admin Side: For administrators to manage the platform.
  • Upload Recorded Video: Upload your conference recordings (Admin Staff only).
  • Download Recorded Videos: Download recorded videos from the database (all users).

How to Chat

To start a chat with another user, follow these steps:

  1. Login to your account.
  2. Go to the chat section available on your dashboard.
  3. Select the user you want to chat with from the list.
  4. Type your message in the input box and press Enter to send.

How to Join a Video Call

To join a video conference call, follow these steps:

  1. Login to your account.
  2. Go to the video call section available on your dashboard.
  3. Click on the "Join Call" button for the ongoing conference you wish to join.
  4. Allow your browser to access your camera and microphone when prompted.
  5. Enjoy your video conference call!
Note: Only users with Admin Staff privileges can upload and download recorded videos. If you encounter any issues or have questions, please contact support.
